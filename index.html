WHO ARE WE?

Daner Engineering was founded in early 2017 and is an independent, and privately owned Danish company.

The company is a brain child of our founders who have more than two decades of experience from the CO2 industry worldwide. In those years, we have handled all aspects of project and site management – from the pre planning stage to the final commissioning of CO2 plants.
Our involvement also covers service and after sales.

Our long standing experience in the CO2 industry gives Daner Engineering an edge in providing our customers in depth solutions to any technical issues related to operation, service and upgrade of existing CO2 plants.
